Guide Information

What Are We Talking About?

Workflowy is a software tool that lets you store information in units called nodes. These nodes appear to be bullet points in a digital outline, and they can be just that. By applying advanced features, however, the nodes can become an interactive network of information that provides actionable insights.

Purpose of This Guide

The purpose of the guide is to help users transform their Workflowy outlines into interactive networks of information. To this end, you will learn four ways in which Workflowy's features can be utilized to achieve compelling project management functionality.

Prerequisite User Knowledge

Infinitely Nesting Nodes

The Nodes in Workflowy are like an infinite Russian Doll. They can nest inside one another to an infinite extent, and this allows the user to deeply categorize and analyze the information at hand.

Breaking Down Projects Into Manageable Steps

“There is only one way to eat an elephant: a bite at a time.” - Desmond Tutu

Follow the steps below to break your project down into manageable tasks.

Step 1: Name Your Project Node

Start by coming up with a name for your project and typing this into a blank node.

Step 2: Come up with intermediate steps and record them

Begin to brainstorm the steps you need to take to accomplish your project end goal by typing items that are nested below your project node. Just let your ideas flow at this point because a digital outliner lets you delete, rephrase or reorder these items later!
